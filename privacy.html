Privacy Policy

App: Guitar Tuner – Free Pro Tuner
Developer: EZ Music Apps

Summary

We do not collect, share, or sell any personal data. The app uses your device’s microphone to analyze live audio and compute pitch on-device. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

Data We Don’t Collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or identifiers
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash analytics
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking

Device Permissions

Microphone: Required to detect pitch. Audio never leaves your device and is not saved.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not include user-generated content, purchases, or social features.

Third Parties

We do not use third‑party SDKs that collect user data. If you email us for support or sponsorship, we will receive the email address and any information you include; we only use it to respond.

Changes

We may update this policy for clarity or to reflect new features. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
